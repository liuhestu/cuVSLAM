Detailed Description

Ground constraint

The ground constraint is a post-processing tool to constrain 3d poses from Odometry on the ground plane. Because of the drifts or failures after some time, 3D poses from odometry will have roll and pitch, even if the robot moves on a flat surface. To resolve it, GroundConstraint will integrate 2d deltas in each frame, removing roll and pitch angles on small deltas.
